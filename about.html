My name is Austin Solache. I'm a web developer creating something useful and informative. With ProjectCars, I wanted to help drivers make the right decision when looking to buy a used vehicle with simple and beginner-friendly guides.

I started this project when I was looking for a vehicle myself. I didn't know if I wanted a truck, SUV, or a sedan. The decision was tough and I had to do intense research through YouTube videos and various car forums. All this infomation was valuable when looking for the perfect used vehicle for me. This infomation saved my thousands by avoiding cars that would break down faster. Also to avoid the scam of purchasing from a dealership with thousands of dollars in meaningless fees.

I want to see my generation break away from the normality of purchasing new vehicles and getting into unnessecary debt when a used vehicle can be just as good but without the thousands in interest.
